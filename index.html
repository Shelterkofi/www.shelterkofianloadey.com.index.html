About Me

Shelter Kofi Anloadey is a dedicated and highly skilled cybersecurity and software engineering professional. With a wealth of experience and a strong educational background, Shelter has developed expertise in various aspects of information technology and security.

Shelter holds multiple certifications from prestigious institutions, including CISCO, EC Council, OPSWAT, and the Open University. These certifications cover a wide range of topics, such as Network Defense, Endpoint Security, Cyber Threat Management, Digital Forensics, and Critical Infrastructure Protection. This diverse knowledge base enables Shelter to approach complex cybersecurity challenges with a comprehensive and well-rounded perspective.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (BTECH) in ICT, Shelter has already completed an HND in ICT from Ho Technical University. This academic journey has equipped Shelter with a solid foundation in ICT principles and practices, further enhanced by practical experience in various projects. Notable projects include the development of a Poultry Farm Management System, Incident Response Simulation and Planning Software, Network Intrusion Detection and Mitigation Software, Standpipe Tracker, and a Meal Planning App. These projects demonstrate Shelter's ability to create innovative solutions that address real-world problems.

In addition to technical prowess, Shelter is committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest advancements in the field. Shelter's dedication to professional growth, coupled with a passion for technology, makes for a standout individual in the cybersecurity and software engineering landscape.
